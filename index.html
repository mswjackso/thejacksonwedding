Travel Information

  • Closest airports
    • SJC (45min from Santa Cruz)
    • SFO (1hr 15min from Santa Cruz)
  • Accommodations
    • Santa cruz is a pretty popular destination, there are a TON of different lodging options in the area. The list below is just a few but there’s lots more, so stay wherever will make your stay most enjoyable. Feel free to call/text michael if you have any questions.
    • Hotels
      • We did not reserve a room block because there are so many hotels to choose from in Santa Cruz. We have looked into a few different options which you can see below to try and fit your respective price ranges. If none of these seem to work for you either then don't worry just google around. Like I said, there are a lot of hotels.
      • Santa Cruz
        • Hampton Inn in Santa Cruz
          Close to downtown Santa Cruz and the freeway, 13 minutes to the venue.
        • Best Western Plus All Suites Inn Santa Cruz
          Close to downtown Santa Cruz and the freeway, 15 minutes to the venue. They will also offer a ~15% discount if you say that you are booking to attend a wedding.
        • Holiday Inn Express Santa Cruz
          Close to downtown Santa Cruz and the freeway, 13 minutes to the venue.
      • Scotts Valley
        • A town nestled in the redwoods next to Santa Cruz.
        • Hilton Scotts Valley
          Close to the freeway and about 10 minutes from the venue.
        • Best Western Scotts Valley
          Close to the freeway and about 16 minutes from the venue.
    • Camping
      • Henry Cowell Redwoods Campground
        Super close to the venue, less than 5 minutes away. Perfect if you want the redwood forest experience.
      • Santa Cruz Redwoods RV resort
        Only 10 minutes from the venue. Perfect if you want the redwood forest experience.
      • New Brighton State Beach Campground
        This is 20 minutes from the venue but allows you to stay very close to the beach if that's what you would like to do.
      • Santa Cruz Harbor RV Park
        This is 20 minutes from the venue but allows you to stay very close to the beach if that's what you would like to do.
    • Others
      • Airbnb - There’s tons of airbnbs around in Santa Cruz and surrounding area, this gives you a lot of options whether you want a cabin, house, close to the beach, downtown, etc.
